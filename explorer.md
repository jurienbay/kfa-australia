# KFA Explorer

![explorer](/assets/explorer.png)

Bushbaby Explorer, also known as the KFA Explorer UL-600

 * [Specification](http://kitplanesforafrica.co.za/_webmoduledata/documents/KFA%20Explorer%20specifications.pdf)

If you value both comfort and adventure, the **Explorer** series is your ideal
companion. These aircraft are available as production-built LSA and UL aircraft
or in kit form. They combine the durability and performance of a true bush
plane with the refinement and comfort you'd expect from a modern aircraft. From
long cross-country journeys to short backcountry hops, the Explorer delivers an
unmatched flying experience.

Specification Synopsis:

 - ASTM F2245-16c certification.
 - Engine Rotax 912ULS at 100HP.
 - Fuel capacity 105L with avg fuel consumption of 18L/hr in the cruise (approx. ~5.8h endurance).
 - V-speeds
    * Vs0  35kt (12* full flap).
    * Vs1  37kt (8* of flap).
    * Vs   42kt
    * Vc   85kt
    * Va   90kt
    * Vno 100kt
    * Vh  105kt
    * Vne 121kt
    * demonstrated xwind 20kt
 - Performance
    * Take-off 80m
    * Landing  90m
    * Climb    800ft/min.
 - Empty Weight 340-365kg (excluding parachute).
 - MTOW 600kg.
 - Baggage capacity/volume - 30kg in 1.0 m^3.
 - Loads 4/-2g.
